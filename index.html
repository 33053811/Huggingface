Dementia & Alzheimer’s

什么是老年痴呆症？

What is Dementia?

老年痴呆症（阿尔茨海默病）是影响记忆、思维和日常功能的一类疾病，常见于老年人。

Dementia, including Alzheimer’s disease, affects memory, thinking, and daily functioning, commonly in older adults.

症状

Symptoms

  • 记忆力下降
  • 语言表达困难
  • 判断力下降
  • 情绪和行为改变
  • Memory loss
  • Difficulty in communication
  • Reduced judgment
  • Mood and behavior changes

预防与照护

Prevention & Care

  • 保持心脑血管健康
  • 规律运动
  • 丰富社交与脑力活动
  • 均衡饮食
  • 充足睡眠
  • Maintain cardiovascular health
  • Regular exercise
  • Social and cognitive activities
  • Balanced diet
  • Adequate sleep
